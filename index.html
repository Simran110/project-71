vist my school

canteen

maked in 2019

Blink and you will miss the entrance to the canteen. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "school canteen" while you are on the main hallway. The canteen has a reasonably good menu at reasonable no prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrations on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep & happening. Hosts the famous "canteen theater fest", which you must attend if possible.

sport gym

maked in 2018

It's a wonderful place. Must visit this place. It is a beautifully maintained small complex with yellow painted walls.
